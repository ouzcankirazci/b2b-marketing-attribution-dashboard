## Attribution comparison
- Google generated the highest same-source first-touch to last-touch won revenue path (Google → Google: 143,954).
- Partner delivered strong revenue efficiency despite lower lead volume, especially through Partner → Partner and Partner → Webinar paths.
- Organic appears to be a strong demand-creation channel because several high-revenue paths start with organic but close through other channels.
- This suggests that first-touch and last-touch should be analyzed together rather than choosing one “correct” attribution model.
- Last-touch alone would understate the contribution of channels that introduce demand earlier in the journey.
